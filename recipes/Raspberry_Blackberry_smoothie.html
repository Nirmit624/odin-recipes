Raspberry Blackberry Smoothie Recipe

Discription

Lets make a delicious smoothie!

Ingredients

  • 1 small banana
  • 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 container vannila yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 4 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place banana, blackberries, raspberries, yogurt, honey, and ice cubes into a blender. Blend until smooth.
